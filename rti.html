Information Policy

Our commitment to transparency and information access.

Right to Information (RTI)

Cross Border Study Network University (CBSN-U) is a private, One Person Company (OPC) and not a public authority as defined under the RTI Act, 2005. Therefore, CBSN-U is not directly covered under the provisions of the RTI Act.

However, in the spirit of transparency and accountability, we are committed to providing information to our partners, students, and the public.

How to Request Information

For any information regarding our operations, policies, or services, you may contact our designated Nodal Officer. Please provide clear details of the information you require.

Request via Email

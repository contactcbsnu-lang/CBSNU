Our Vision

Our vision is to build an "ideal education center" that makes quality education affordable and accessible for everyone. We want every child to have an equal opportunity to learn, regardless of their family's financial circumstances.

We will achieve this goal through these core principles:

  • Supportive Examinations and Scholarships: Our exam and scholarship system is designed not just to test students, but to provide them with the financial and academic support they need to move forward.
  • Low-Cost Education: Our core principle, embedded in tools like Vidya AI, is to provide the best study materials at the lowest possible cost.
  • Opportunities for All: Our scholarship plans are specifically designed to create opportunities for young students whose parents cannot bear the burden of expensive tuition.
  • Future Education Centers: In the future, CBSN-U will establish its own coaching centers and schools. These will not be "hifi" institutions, but rather "ideal" educational hubs that provide better facilities at lower fees.

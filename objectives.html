Our Objectives

To realize our vision, we are focused on a phased plan, separating our immediate goals from our long-term ambitions.

Immediate Objectives (Phase 1)

Our primary and immediate goal is to establish CBSN-U as the premier, trusted partner for schools, colleges, and institutes. We aim to immediately begin:

  • Conducting secure, transparent, and international-standard examinations for our partner institutions.
  • Building a robust franchise network to collaborate with independent coaching centers.
  • Providing a world-class, free Test Practice Centre (TPC) for all students.

Long-Term Objectives (Phase 2)

Our future growth is focused on building a complete educational ecosystem by:

  • Developing Scholarship Programs: To launch and scale a comprehensive scholarship program, providing tangible financial and academic opportunities to deserving students who face economic barriers.
  • Launching AI-Powered Tools: To fully develop and deploy Vidya AI as a leading low-cost platform for aggregating free educational resources, alongside The Textbook Helper to break down language and complexity barriers.
  • Establishing Physical Centers: To first establish a network of CBSN-U coaching centers, followed by the launch of CBSN-U schools, all founded on the principle of providing high-quality facilities at affordable fees.

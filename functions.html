Our Functions

To achieve our objectives, CBSN-U performs the following key functions:

Exam Management

We design, manage, and conduct secure, scalable, and transparent examinations for our partner institutions and schools.

Franchise Operations

We manage and support a network of independent coaching collaborations, providing them with our tools and testing platform.

AI Tool Development

Our team actively builds, trains, and maintains our AI-powered learning tools, including Vidya AI and The Textbook Helper.

Scholarship Processing

We are responsible for the entire scholarship lifecycle, from managing applications to fairly distributing funds to deserving students.

Content Creation

We create and curate high-quality educational content, including summaries and translations for The Textbook Helper.

Platform Maintenance

We ensure our Test Practice Centre (TPC) and all digital platforms are operational, secure, and available 24/7 for students.
